Section

Basic usage

This component is a wrapper over the content that adds a border, padding, and padding between elements You can also change styles by attaching classes to components and specifying the necessary styles (see example Custom styles)
  • pupa-section-container - container for your content
  • pupa-section-title container for the header, positions the elements at the edges
  • pupa-section-sub-title container for subheading, positions elements at the edges
